A Botswana born business built on trust, quality, and a genuine passion for vehicles

Kenosi Motors was founded with a simple mission: to provide exceptional service and quality vehicles to the people of Botswana. Over the years, we have grown into a trusted name in the automotive industry, known for our commitment to customer satisfaction and integrity.

We stock a diverse range of vehicles — from reliable everyday hatchbacks to powerful 4x4s and luxury sedans — ensuring that whether you are a first-time buyer or an experienced motorist, we have the right car for you.
